# Findings

## Research & Discoveries
- **Project Concept:** German learning web app for children.
- **Identified Core Features:**
  - File Upload (images, books, PDF, Word, presentations).
  - AI Module (search content, ask questions).
  - Creative Tool (generate learning cards, videos, audio via Google NotebookLM).
  - Authentication (Login Panel).
  - Tiered Subscription (Cost Free: 1 picture upload, Full License: no limits).

## Discovery Questionnaire Answers
1. **North Star:** A highly effective learning Web App for Gymnasium students (5th grade+), enabling them to study from school materials, which parents are willing to pay for. Starting with German, English, Math, and expanding later.
2. **Integrations:** Google NotebookLM and Supabase. (API keys pending).
3. **Source of Truth:** Supabase (via MCP). (Account/API pending).
4. **Delivery Payload:** Vercel. (Workspace/API pending).
5. **Behavioral Rules:** Absolutely no vulgar, sexual, criminal, or aggressive language. Texts and UI must be child-friendly, intuitive, and ergonomic.

## Constraints
- Web application must be entirely in German.
- BLAST Protocol invariants must be respected at all times.
